Pig Health Diagnostic Tool

An AI-powered application for diagnosing pig health conditions through image analysis and symptom recognition

View on GitHub Try Demo

Key Features

Image Analysis

Upload images of pigs for AI-powered disease detection using advanced computer vision techniques.

Symptom Recognition

Input observed symptoms to receive accurate disease diagnosis and treatment recommendations.

AI-Powered

Utilizes deep learning models trained on extensive datasets for accurate disease detection.

Supported Diseases

Swine Flu

Highly contagious respiratory disease caused by influenza viruses.

PRRS

Porcine Reproductive and Respiratory Syndrome affecting breeding stock.

Foot and Mouth Disease

Viral disease affecting cloven-hoofed animals.

Pneumonia

Respiratory infection affecting lung tissue.

Swine Dysentery

Severe inflammatory disease of the large intestine.

Sarcoptic Mange

Skin disease caused by parasitic mites.

Swine Erysipelas

Bacterial infection causing diamond-shaped skin lesions.

Porcine Parvovirus

Viral infection affecting reproductive performance.
