How to Stop Hackers: A Comprehensive Guide

Learn essential cybersecurity practices to protect yourself and your systems from malicious attacks

Key Cybersecurity Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts
  • Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible
  • Keep all software and systems up to date
  • Use antivirus and anti-malware software
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and suspicious emails
  • Regularly backup important data
  • Use secure networks and avoid public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks

Common Attack Vectors

Phishing

Deceptive emails and messages designed to steal sensitive information.

Malware

Malicious software designed to harm or exploit computer systems.

Ransomware

Malware that encrypts files and demands payment for decryption.

Social Engineering

Manipulating people to divulge confidential information.
